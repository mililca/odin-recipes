Russian Salad

Traditional Russian Salad you can serve on any holiday table or as on it's own.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Mayonnaise
  • Potato
  • Eggs
  • Peas

  1. Cut chicken into cubes, boil and add to the bowl.
  2. Cut potatoes and boiled eggs into cubes and add to the bowl.
  3. Add peas, mayonnaise.
  4. Season with salt and pepper and mix well.
