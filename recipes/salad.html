Salad

Refreshing and easy to make homemade Caesar Salad.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Caesar Sauce
  • Optional: Avocado
  • Optional: Cucumber

  1. Cut chicken in small cubes and fry in a pan until cooked.
  2. Cut lettuce and add to the bowl.
  3. Optional: Cut avocado and cucumber and add to the bowl.
  4. Bake bread cubes in the oven until cooked.
  5. Mix together in a bowl with caesar sauce.
