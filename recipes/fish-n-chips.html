Fish-n-Chips

Easy to make fish-n-chips, except you don't have to do anything.

Ingredients

  • Frozen fish steaks
  • Frozen fries

  1. Fry frozen fish steaks on a pan with no oil.
  2. Fry French fries in a lot of oil in a pot.
  3. Season the fish steaks to your liking with lemon juice and pepper.
